About Us

The connection of the Juma family with gastronomy goes back to Italy in the 19th century, in Brazil, beginning in 1974, Mr Fabrizio Juma founded Fatec Food in Itu. After years of success, the restaurant went from one to four units After almost 47 years, Fabrizio runs the Fatec Food restaurant at Rua Floriano Peixoto, 558 – Centro, his daughters, Paola and Andrea, continue to run this famous restaurant.
